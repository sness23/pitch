The Problem

R&D lives in two places (papers & proteins), but tools don't

Fragmented Stack

PDFs, ELN notes, viewers, docking/MD, ad-hoc scripts create brittle hand-offs and lost rationale across 8-12 tools.

Volume Shock

~243k PDB entries and >200M AlphaFold predictions bury signal in noise, making discovery overwhelming.

Time Pressure

Rising R&D costs ($2.23B per asset) and pressure to make faster, better decisions every month.
